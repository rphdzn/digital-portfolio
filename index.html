DIGITAL PORTFOLIO

Hello there! I am Raphael Dizon.

I am a multimedia artist, focusing on graphic design, photography, branding, and illustrating. I look at the world through a creative lens. I aim to use my skills not only for my creative expression, but to help solve problems related to visual design.

Here are some of my works!

Cafe de Vivre Cup Illustration


Cafe de Vivre is a coffee shop located in Abreeza Mall, Davao City, committed to an experience that honors art and craftsmanship. These illustrations are presented on their to-go coffee cups, reflecting the seasons, mood, and aesthetic of the cafe.

Kiel's Coffee Logo Redesign


This is the logo redesign of Kiel's Coffee. Kiel's Coffee is a cafe located in Davao City, Philippines. The objective of this redesign was to simply update the logo while retaining its visual character.

CHROMA Magazine


CHROMA is a magazine project done for our Multimedia Publishing course. It is a magazine dedicated to artists, by artists.
